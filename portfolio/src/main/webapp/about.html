An Introduction to me

“Success consists of moving from failure to failure without a loss of enthusiasm.”
-Often attributed to Winston Churchill
This is a quote that continues to help me to this day.

I'm 27 now, and I spent my early 20's working for an Israeli software company that developed a specialized system for the Diamond industry, that manages inventory, Accounting, and some customer relationship management or CRM tasks.

The company gave me responsibilities that included SQL business intelligence report development, sales, and implementation of the software; I learned all of this on the job.

I traveled to countries around the world where I worked with clients to setup, troubleshoot the software, and build custom SSRS reports, keeping up with my tasks in the different time zones.
I learned a lot and I got to travel the world.
I held this position for four years.

When the company opened a New York office, I was asked to help run it. I returned to New York self-assured, yet one piece was missing. Although I had honed many skills and built rapport with customers, I wanted education. So, I decided I was ready to return to an academic environment.
I left my position in 2018 and enrolled at the Borough of Manhattan Community College.
Over the last two years I have continued to build on my knowledge, gained new friends, and have matured in ways that leave me inspired for the future.
I seek to further my career in computer science and mathematics, and the opportunity to pursue a challenging environment, and to work with others at the top of my field.
